## Weekly Grocery Shopping: Add Cavendish Banana to Cart

This skill adds one Cavendish banana to the cart on Woolworths.com.au.

1. **Precondition:** Ensure you are on the Woolworths homepage (www.woolworths.com.au).
2. In the search bar at the top of the page (labeled "Search products, recipes and ideas"), type "Cavendish banana".
3. Click the "Search button".
4. Locate the first item, "Cavendish bananas each" (the yellow ones that are 81 cents), and click its "Add to cart" button.
5. Confirm the item has been added to the cart.
